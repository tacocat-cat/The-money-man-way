Lesson 1: Understanding the Importance of Multiple Income Streams

Example:

  • Research: Dive deep into financial literature like "The Richest Man in Babylon," "Your Money or Your Life," and online resources such as Investopedia.
  • Self-Assessment: Conduct a thorough analysis of your current income sources, categorizing them into active and passive.
  • Documentation: Create a comprehensive spreadsheet or financial dashboard to visually represent your income streams, expenses, and savings.

By the end of this lesson, you should have a clear understanding of why having multiple income streams is crucial for financial stability and how to assess your current financial situation.
